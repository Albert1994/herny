## Цель

Понимать enterprise architecture frameworks — структура для управления архитектурой на уровне организации.

---

## Ключевые концепции

### TOGAF ADM (Architecture Development Method)
Фазы:
1. **Preliminary** — framework, principles
2. **Architecture Vision** — scope, stakeholders
3. **Business Architecture**
4. **Information Systems Architecture** (Data + Application)
5. **Technology Architecture**
6. **Opportunities & Solutions**
7. **Migration Planning**
8. **Implementation Governance**
9. **Architecture Change Management**

### Architecture Domains (TOGAF)
- Business Architecture
- Data Architecture
- Application Architecture
- Technology Architecture

### Other frameworks
| Framework | Focus |
|-----------|-------|
| **Zachman** | Classification matrix (what, how, where, who, when, why) |
| **FEAF** | US federal EA |
| **SAFe** | Agile at scale + EA |
| **Gartner** | Practical EA, not prescriptive |

### Architecture Governance
- Architecture Review Board (ARB)
- Compliance assessments
- Architecture principles (max 10–15, memorable)
- Standards and reference architectures

### Когда TOGAF нужен
- Enterprise 500+ employees
- Regulated industries
- Many legacy systems
- Не нужен для startup 10 человек
