# Портфолио кейсов

**Этап:** [[00 Обзор этапа|5 — Практика архитектора]]  
**Время:** ongoing

---

## Цель

Собрать portfolio architecture case studies — доказательство компетенций для hiring, promotion, или consulting.

---

## Структура case study

```markdown
# [Project Name]

## Context
- Company/domain, team size, constraints
- Business problem

## Role
- Your responsibilities as architect

## Requirements
- Functional (3–5 bullet points)
- Non-functional with metrics

## Architecture
- C4 Context + Container diagrams
- Key ADRs (links or inline)

## Technical decisions
- Why this stack/style
- Trade-offs considered
- Alternatives rejected

## Challenges
- What went wrong or was hard
- How you resolved it

## Results
- Metrics: latency, uptime, delivery speed
- Business impact if known

## Lessons learned
- What you'd do differently
```

---

## Рекомендуемые 3 кейса

### Case 1: Greenfield system
- New product from scratch
- Show: requirements → architecture → implementation
- Ideal: modular monolith or microservices with clear boundaries

### Case 2: Migration / modernization
- Legacy → modern architecture
- Show: strangler fig, risk management, incremental delivery
- Ideal: monolith to microservices or on-prem to cloud

### Case 3: Scale / performance
- System under load
- Show: bottleneck analysis, caching, sharding, observability
- Ideal: 10x traffic growth handled

---

## Где публиковать

- GitHub repo с `/docs/architecture/`
- Notion / Obsidian publish
- Personal blog
- LinkedIn articles (summary + link)

## Confidentiality

- Anonymize company names if needed
- Use fictional names for sensitive details
- Focus on architecture patterns, not proprietary business logic
